Privacy Policy

This explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide when booking (name, email, and phone number if provided).
  • Scheduling details (lesson time, time zone, and notes you submit).
  • Payment information is processed by Stripe; we do not store full card numbers.

How We Use Information

  • To provide lessons and communicate about bookings.
  • To send Zoom links and confirmations.
  • To provide customer support and handle refunds/rescheduling.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers needed to run the business (e.g., Calendly for scheduling, Zoom for lessons, and Stripe for payments) and as required by law.

Data Retention

We keep booking records for business purposes and to resolve issues. You may request deletion of your data where legally applicable.
